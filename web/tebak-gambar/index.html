📋 Detail Game

  • 200 level dibagi jadi 10 BAB tematik (20 level per bab) — biar gak monoton
  • Sistem koin: jawab bener +25 koin, dapet bintang 3 +50 bonus. Hint pake koin
  • 3 hint: Buka 1 huruf (25💰), Buang huruf salah (40💰), Skip level (100💰)
  • Bintang: 3⭐ = jawab tanpa hint · 2⭐ = pake 1-2 hint · 1⭐ = pake banyak hint/skip
  • Auto-save: progress disimpan di browser pake localStorage (gak hilang refresh)
  • Mobile-first: tampilan dioptimasi buat HP, tap-tap nyaman
  • Anti-cheat: jawaban di-hash, gak bisa intip lewat inspect element
